Welcome to Flo-Music

The art of making music has been around at least 35,000 years and it is still a practice that many if not all cultures participate in today. It has evolved from just stricly instumentation to now adding digital sound with the evolution of technology. What once took years of learning how to play intruments, getting in a band or orchestra to create a united sound, or learning how to conduct such things, we now can do these things with electonic sounds and instruments in the comfort of our own home. The goal for this website is to educate about the hobby of music and get you to get started making beats, song writing, and even recording.

Wecome to Central Illinois Christians In Mission 

Central Illinois Christians in Mission, Inc. provides our neighbors in the Decatur area with household goods. We've served over 1400 families each of the past four years, providing everything from cereal bowls to refrigerators at no cost. Clients are referred to us by social organizations, schools, and churches in the area. The abundance that we share is donated to us by the people and organizations in our community. We have no revenue stream, so we depend upon the generosity of the community for monetary donations which we use to pay our operating costs. No one at the mission is paid, so any donation sustains our goal of assisting community members in need.
